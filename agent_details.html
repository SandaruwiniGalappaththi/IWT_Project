About This Gig

Travel agents are responsible for helping members of the public select and organise their ideal holiday on a limited budget. A travel agent's role is to help people plan, choose and arrange their holiday. They will usually work to a budget set out by whoever is planning the holiday.They also offer advice and opinions on where to go and local tourist attractions, events and customs. Travel agents sell transportation, lodging, and admission to entertainment activities to individuals and groups planning trips.
